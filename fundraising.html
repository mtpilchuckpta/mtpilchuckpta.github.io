Fundraising

Please know that we do not expect every family to participate in every fundraiser!
Our hope is that every student will be able to do something to support our work.
We think providing a variety of options is the best way to do that.

Direct donationWrite a check (or donate online) and spend the rest of the year serenely ignoring every fundraising request!
Klesick'sOrder a box (or more) of produce Klesick's by August 31st, and 10% of the sales order comes to Mt Pilchuck PTA
Popcorn CardNew this year, purchase a $5 popcorn card and your student will get free popcorn at every Popcorn Friday all year long! Dates are TBA, but we guarantee at least 5 Popcorn Fridays.
Box Tops for EducationScan Box Tops receipts on items you already buy using the new Box Top app and designate proceeds to Mt Pilchuck PTA.
Rewards programsMake Mt Pilchuck Elementary PTA your beneficiary on Amazon Smile, Fred Meyer Rewards, or United Way.
Catalog Sale
(fall)		Students sell items from catalog to family and friends and we get a portion of the profits.
Apex Fun Run
(spring)		Students will spend two weeks experiencing a fabulous leadership program. The culmination of the two weeks is a fundraising run.
Silver Graphics
(spring)		Sales of custom items featuring your student's own art
Altitude Trampoline NightAttend Mt Pilchuck night at Altitude Trampoline park and a percentage of your entry fee is donated to Mt Pilchuck PTA.
