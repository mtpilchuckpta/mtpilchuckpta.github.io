Frequently asked questions about yearbook
How do I order a yearbook? The pre-order period for the 2019-20 yearbook was the first two weeks of June.
When will I get my yearbook? Yearbooks were shipped on August 24th. If you ordered online for home delivery and your yearbook has not arrived, please refer to your receipt from the publisher.

If you ordered for pickup, the books have arrived. You may pick them up from the school office.
Is it too late to get a 2019-20 yearbook? My kid really wants one. We had a limited number of extra copies printed, and will be making these copies available to purchase at a cost of $15 each.
To pre-pay for one or more copies, please visit our store to purchase. We will email you when your book is ready to be picked up at school.

If you are not able to pay online, let us know and we'll see what can be worked out about connecting in person for a cash sale.
