Fundraising

Please know that we do not expect every family to participate in every fundraiser!
Our hope is that every student will be able to do something to support our work.
We think providing a variety of options is the best way to do that.

Direct donation Write a check (or donate online) and spend the rest of the year serenely ignoring every fundraising request!
Box Tops for Education Scan Box Tops receipts on items you already buy using the new Box Top app and designate proceeds to Mt Pilchuck PTA.
Rewards programs Make Mt Pilchuck Elementary PTA your beneficiary on Amazon Smile, Fred Meyer Rewards, or United Way.
Catalog Sale
(fall)		 Students sell items from catalog to family and friends and we get a portion of the profits.
Apex Fun Run
(spring)		 Students will spend two weeks experiencing a fabulous leadership program. The culmination of the two weeks is a fundraising run.
Silver Graphics
(spring)		 Sales of custom items featuring your student's own art
